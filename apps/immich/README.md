# Immich

A high-performance, self-hosted photo and video management solution that serves as a direct alternative to Google Photos. It offers automatic mobile backup, AI-powered search and organization, and a polished web interface for browsing your entire media library.

## Key Features

- **Auto backup** — Automatically back up photos and videos from iOS and Android devices
- **AI features** — Face recognition, object detection, and smart search powered by machine learning
- **Map view** — Explore your photos on an interactive map based on GPS metadata
- **Sharing** — Share albums and individual photos with family and friends
